Peanut Chutney

Description

Yummy peanut chutney for idli and dose.

Ingredients

  • Peanuts
  • Red Chilli
  • Grated Coconut
  • Garlic

  • Salt
  • Cooking oil
  • Mustard Seeds
  • Curry leaves

Steps

  1. Roast and de-skin groundnut seeds.
  2. Add groundnut seeds to blender, along with garlic, coconut, salt, red chillies.
  3. Temper mustard seeds and curry leaves and mix with blended paste.
